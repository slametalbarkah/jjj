Kuku Emmanuel Fidelis (born 10 March 1999) is a Nigerian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Portuguese club Vizela .

Playing career

[ edit ]

Fidelis is a youth product of the Nigerian Gee-Lec Academy , before moving to the academies of Vizela , and Porto . After starting his senior career with Vizela, he transferred to Boavista on 31 August 2020. [ 1 ] He made his professional debut with Boavista in a 2–0 Primeira Liga win over F.C. Paços de Ferreira on 16 April 2021. [ 2 ]

International career

[ edit ]

Fidelis represented the Nigeria U17s for 2017 Africa U-17 Cup of Nations qualification matches. [ 3 ]
